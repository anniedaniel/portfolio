About

My name is Annie Daniel and I love the Internet.

I am a student at The University of North Carolina at Chapel Hill's School of Journalism and Mass Communication studying multimedia journalism. I also have a second major in political science.

I am a journalist looking for the best way to tell a story. My objective is to dig out the story that everyone missed and bring it to life.

I am a web developer always learning and making something new.

I am an advocate for hacker journalists in every newsroom who are able to build beautiful web spaces while remaining conscious of the ethics and principles of journalism as an acting member of the press. I am also an advocate of government transparency and accessibility of data, resources and government activities.
